About Nearby Medicine Finder

Nearby Medicine Finder is a smart web application designed to help users find the availability of medicines in nearby medical stores.

Our platform uses advanced geolocation technology to show results within a 1 km range, making it a quick and efficient tool during emergencies when every minute counts.

Local Search

Find medicines available in stores near your current location with our precise geolocation technology.

Real-time Data

Get up-to-date information on medicine availability to save time and avoid unnecessary trips.

Community Focus

Designed with senior citizens and emergency situations in mind to make healthcare accessible.

Our Mission

We aim to revolutionize how people access essential medicines by bridging the gap between medical store inventory and patient needs. Our mission is to assist patients, especially senior citizens and those in emergency situations, in locating life-saving medications without the stress of running from store to store.

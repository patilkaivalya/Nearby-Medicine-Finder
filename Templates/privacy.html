Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Location Data: We collect your approximate location to find nearby medical stores, but we don't store this information permanently.
  • Search Queries: Medicine names you search for are used to provide relevant results.
  • Device Information: Basic device data helps us optimize the app for different devices.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide accurate search results for nearby medicine availability
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • For analytics to understand usage patterns (anonymous data)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (like mapping providers) that have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing these policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll notify users of significant changes through our website or app.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@nearbymedicinefinder.com.
